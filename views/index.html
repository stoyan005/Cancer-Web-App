Welcome to Cancer Awareness!

This web app helps you learn about cancer prevention and assess your personal risk level based on lifestyle and family history.

All information is kept secure and nothing gets shared anywhere. This is purely for awareness and educational purposes.

How the Risk Test Works

Our test evaluates your risk based on factors like age, smoking habits, family history, and diet. After completing the test, you'll receive a personalized risk assessment along with advice to improve your health.

Cancer Prevention Tips

  • Maintain a healthy diet: Include fruits, vegetables, and whole grains in your meals.
  • Avoid smoking: Smoking greatly increases the risk of many cancers.
  • Exercise regularly: Aim for at least 30 minutes of physical activity most days.
  • Get screened: Regular screenings can detect cancer early when treatment is more effective.
  • Limit alcohol: Excessive alcohol consumption can increase cancer risk.
  • Protect your skin: Use sunscreen and avoid prolonged sun exposure.
